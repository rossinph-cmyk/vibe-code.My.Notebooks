## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Voice Recordings:** When you use the voice-to-text feature, your voice recordings are temporarily processed to transcribe them into text. These recordings are sent to OpenAI for transcription and are not stored on our servers.

**Text Notes:** All notes, notebooks, and text content you create are stored locally on your device.

**Photos and Images:** When you choose to add background images to notebooks or your home screen, these images are stored locally on your device. We request access to your photo library solely for this purpose.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:** We may collect information about your device including device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improvement purposes.

**Usage Data:** We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how the app is used and improve user experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

**Voice Transcription:** Voice recordings are sent to OpenAI to convert speech to text. OpenAI processes these recordings according to their privacy policy.

**Local Storage:** All your notes, notebooks, settings, and images are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

**App Functionality:** We use your preferences and settings to provide personalized features like dark mode, custom colors, and background images.

**Service Improvement:** Anonymous usage data helps us identify bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.

Required to save your notes, notebooks, settings, and selected images locally on your device.

## 6. Data Security

**Local Storage:** Your notes and data are stored locally using industry-standard secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS.

**Encryption:** Data stored on your device is protected by iOS encryption and security features.

**No Account Required:** The app currently does not require account creation, reducing the risk of data breaches.

**Transmission Security:** Voice recordings are transmitted to OpenAI using encrypted HTTPS connections with industry-standard TLS encryption.

**Access Controls:** Only you have access to your device data. We do not have remote access to your local data.

**Regular Security Updates:** We regularly update our app to address security vulnerabilities and implement best practices.

---

## 7. Data Retention

**Local Data:** Your notes, notebooks, and settings remain on your device until you delete the app or manually delete the content.

**Voice Recordings:** Temporary voice recordings are immediately sent to OpenAI for transcription and are not stored by us. OpenAI may retain recordings according to their retention policy.

**Usage Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics are retained for up to 24 months for analysis and service improvement.

**Deletion:** You can delete individual notes, entire notebooks, or uninstall the app to remove all data from your device.
